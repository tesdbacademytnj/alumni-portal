Our Story

Built for Institute Communities

AlumniPortal was created to solve a real problem: talented graduates from our partner institutes were unable to efficiently connect with opportunities and each other. We built a platform that makes those connections simple, structured, and accessible.

Today, we serve students and alumni from SSS, Sainora, SakthiDB Technology, and TesDB Academy — with plans to expand further across Tamil Nadu and India.
